# General Overview

!!! info "Distribution Model"
    Of the 55,783,940,100 total supply: **77.57%** flows through Node-Based Distribution (ONE venture, ONE care, community grants, developer incentives, Node referrals, operator rewards, and the Founding Contributors Royalty). The remaining **22.43%** goes to Direct Mint Channels (liquidity mining, impermanent loss protection, verified users, and mystery boxes). Direct channels let users mint EXPL based on ecosystem engagement — no Node required.

---

## Node Operators and Distribution

Node operators are the only participants who can mint EXPL through the main distribution contracts. Each mint triggers an automated split: tokens flow to designated non-custodial escrows (ONE venture, ONE care, community grants, developer incentives) while operators retain a fixed portion as their reward.

Every allocation ratio is enforced by smart contract — operators cannot redirect tokens.

---

## Founding Contributors Royalty

The Founding Contributors Royalty allocates **771,000,000 EXPL** (1.38% of total supply) to founding contributors through gradual Node-based distribution. Each of the 100,000 EXPL Nodes generates an additional **+10 EXPL daily** designated as founding royalty.

This model replaces the original TNO Cards program (now [archived](../archive/overview.md)):

- **Gradual distribution** instead of one-time minting
- **Reduced sell pressure** through sustained, daily-paced release
- **Aligned incentives** — founders benefit as the Node network grows
