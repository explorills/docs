# How to Mint TNO Cards

## Desktop Browser Method

Mint directly from a desktop browser using a Web3 wallet extension.

### Requirements

- :material-laptop: Desktop computer with Chrome, Firefox, or Brave browser
- :material-wallet: Web3 wallet extension installed (MetaMask recommended)
    - [MetaMask Extension Link](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/metamask/nkbihfbeogaeaoehlefnkodbefgpgknn){:target="_blank"}
- :material-currency-usd: Sufficient cryptocurrency balance for minting + gas fees

---

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Navigate to your preferred TNO Card tier:**
    - [**node.expl.one/bronze**](https://node.expl.one/bronze){:target="_blank"}
    - [**node.expl.one/silver**](https://node.expl.one/silver){:target="_blank"}
    - [**node.expl.one/gold**](https://node.expl.one/gold){:target="_blank"}
    - [**node.expl.one/diamond**](https://node.expl.one/diamond){:target="_blank"}

2. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
3. Choose network (Ethereum or Flare)
4. Select the quantity of TNO Cards
5. Click the **"MINT"** button
6. Confirm the transaction in your wallet extension
7. Wait for the transaction to confirm
8. Verify ownership by clicking **"My Address Info"**

---

## Mobile Device Method

Mint from a smartphone or tablet using a Web3-enabled mobile wallet.

### Requirements

- :material-cellphone: iOS or Android smartphone or tablet
- :material-wallet-outline: Web3-compatible mobile wallet app
- :material-currency-usd: Sufficient cryptocurrency balance for minting + gas fees

---

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Open your Web3 mobile wallet
2. Navigate to the in-app browser
3. **Enter your preferred TNO Card tier URL:**
    - [**node.expl.one/bronze**](https://node.expl.one/bronze){:target="_blank"}
    - [**node.expl.one/silver**](https://node.expl.one/silver){:target="_blank"}
    - [**node.expl.one/gold**](https://node.expl.one/gold){:target="_blank"}
    - [**node.expl.one/diamond**](https://node.expl.one/diamond){:target="_blank"}

4. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
5. Choose network (Ethereum or Flare)
6. Select the quantity of TNO Cards
7. Click the **"MINT"** button
8. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
9. Wait for the transaction to confirm
10. Verify ownership by clicking **"My Address Info"**
