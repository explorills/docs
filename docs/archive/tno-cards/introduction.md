!!! danger "Archived — Program Discontinued"

## Original Program Overview

TNO Cards were a temporary mechanism that allowed early adopters to acquire EXPL before the main launch. The program was limited to 771,000,000 EXPL — 1.38% of total supply — and was intended to fund the initial liquidity pool.

## What Replaced TNO Cards

The 1.38% supply allocation has been restructured as the **Founding Contributors Royalty**, distributed gradually through EXPL Nodes:

- **100,000 EXPL Nodes** each generate **+10 EXPL daily** as founding contributors royalty
- Distribution spans **731 days**, ensuring gradual and sustainable token release
- Eliminates sell pressure from large one-time mints
- Total allocation remains **771,000,000 EXPL** (1.38% of total supply)
