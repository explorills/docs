## Tiered pricing model

Prices are denominated in USD. Payment is made in the native currency of the chosen network (ETH on Ethereum, FLR on Flare) at the current exchange rate.

### Tier 1: Nodes 1 -- 1,000

- **Starting price:** $1
- **Price increase:** +$1 per 100 Nodes
