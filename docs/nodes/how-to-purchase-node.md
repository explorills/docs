# How to Purchase Node

## Desktop Browser Method

Mint directly from the preferred web browser using a Web3 wallet extension.

### Requirements

- :material-laptop: Desktop computer with Chrome, Firefox, or Brave browser
- :material-wallet: Web3 wallet extension installed (MetaMask recommended)
  - [MetaMask Extension Link](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/metamask/nkbihfbeogaeaoehlefnkodbefgpgknn){:target="_blank"}
- :material-currency-usd: Sufficient cryptocurrency balance for minting + gas fees

---

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Visit **[node.expl.one/#EXPL-Nodes](https://node.expl.one/#EXPL-Nodes){:target="_blank"}**
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
3. Choose network (Ethereum or Flare)
4. Select the quantity of EXPL Nodes
5. Click the **"MINT"** button
6. Wait for the MINT queue
7. Confirm transaction in Web3 wallet extension
8. Wait until transaction is confirmed
9. Verify ownership by clicking **"My Address Info"**
10. Click on any Node ID to view your NFT on the corresponding blockchain explorer

---

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Open your Web3 mobile wallet application
2. Navigate to the browser function within your wallet app
3. Enter the URL: **[node.expl.one/#EXPL-Nodes](https://node.expl.one/#EXPL-Nodes){:target="_blank"}**
4. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
5. Choose network (Ethereum or Flare)
6. Choose the quantity of EXPL Nodes
7. Click the **"MINT"** button
8. Wait for the MINT queue
9. Confirm transaction in Web3 wallet
10. Wait until transaction is confirmed
11. Verify ownership by clicking **"My Address Info"**
12. Click on any Node ID to view your NFT on the corresponding blockchain explorer
